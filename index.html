Gisèle cares

I

Innovation

Together provide authentic, resilient, curious and humble leadership that brings confidence among postgrads while inspiring new ideas through use of emerging technologies, video, text, audio and images in GSU communication and class representatives electronic elections.

Together lead self and others by attracting resources to support and protect teams by building a case for grassroots innovation by partnering with Alumni and support student entrepreneurship which supports SDG No. 17: Partnerships.

Together define scope of challenges, foster a culture of innovation and model behaviours that supports improvement of consumption of student services through developing innovative awards for students who identify solutions to student challenges.

C

Community

Together carefully plan, define purpose and prepare GSU events that cater for a broader virtual audience of EU and Non-EU students.

Together equitably incorporate a diverse range of student voices, ideas and information to lay groundwork for quality outcomes and demoractic legitimacy of class representatives and convenors.

Together support and encourage postgraduates, college, government and other stakeholders to contribute to the hardship fund and improve student welfare through collaboration and a shared purpose.

Together listen to each other, explore new ideas, learn and apply information in ways that generate new options for postgraduate counselling services.

Together develop a clear and open process in the management of the GSU and provide a public record of the event organization, sponsors, outcomes and range of views and ideas expressed on a weekly, monthly, quarterly and annual basis.

Together emphasize the need for impact and action by ensuring that each student's effort has a real potential to make a difference and create awareness of that potential through establishing an award for student contributions in various thematic areas.

Together promote a sustained culture of participation by organizing events with college and other stakeholders to support the ongoing quality of engagement by supporting UN SDG No. 11: sustainable cities and communities.

A

Accessibility

Together ensure that communication from TCD and GSU to postgraduates on various services is perceivable, simpler language for students from non-English speaking countries, time-based, distinguishable, easy to comprehend and adaptable to the current uncertain times.

Together revamp the GSU website and social media channels to enhance user interface components, navigation and improve operability of GSU digital engagement in a virtual setting.

Together ensure postgraduates understand online services such as library, Trinity sports, spaces, counselling, research, attend lectures, webinars and blackboard in a readable, predictable and provide assistance.

Together advocate for a robust online academic and research content sufficient to be interpreted reliably by all postgraduates and supported through assistive technologies. As a result supports SDG No. 4: Quality Education.

R

Responsibility

Together embrace a personal and professional approach to ethics in treating student data privacy in a virtual environment during case work, class representative and convenor elections.

Together be accountable to the GSU executive committee, recruitment procedures, planning, objectives and task execution through regular reporting, audit and compliance with industry standards.

Together work with PhD Workers Rights, teaching assistants and post-graduate volunteers to ensure they are treated equally and respectfully. Therefore advocate for the right to association, expression and access to grievance mechanisms related to wages, working hours, home working, health and safety.

Together care for the environment by minimizing pollution and embracing sustainable production and consumption of TCD services which supports SDG No. 12: responsible consumption and production.

Together benchmark with top 100 international universities graduate student union performance monitoring, evaluation and implementing feedback.

Together champion the 17 United Nations Sustainable Development Goals to transform GSU and TCD especially with regards to food waste, plastic, water and air pollution which is covered under SDG goal 2, 6 and 15.

E

Equality

Together be guided by United Nations principles on human rights and recognize that each postgraduate is born free and equal in dignity and rights, and endowed with reason and conscience and should act towards one another in the spirit of respect.

Together ensure that every postgraduate is not discriminated by race, colour, sex, language, religion, political or other opinion, national or social origin, property, birth or status through representing students voices in equality committees.

Together upscale counselling services to victims of cruelty, inhuman or degrading treatment or punishment.

Together represent students in college tribunals when fundamental rights are violated under the Irish laws.
